Physical Space

The kit was made to support your creative process on IoT projects that involve physical spaces.

You normally should start with the context and a representation of the physical space in your project or idea.

The blueprints provided in the kit are generic scenarios to kick-start your creativity.

User Journeys

Looking at the blueprint you have the bigr picture of the possible user-journeys.

Focus on the problem you need to solve and look at it from a journey prespective, single steps and interactions that develop along the journey.

Interactions

Think in journeys and small interactions triggered by sensors, devices and services by using the cards.

Service Journey

Keep ideating, and map your ideas and interactions directly on the blueprint. Make use of the cards ad the 3D Printed pieces.

Play around with the devices, test your ideas and role play. This level of accessibility will help on the discussion no matter who is in the room.
